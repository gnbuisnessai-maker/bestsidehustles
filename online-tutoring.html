What is Online Tutoring?

Online tutoring involves teaching students subjects like math, science, languages, music, and more through video conferencing and online platforms. You set your own rates, schedule, and subject matter, making it highly flexible.

Getting Started

  • Choose subjects you're knowledgeable and passionate about
  • Get relevant certifications or degrees (helpful but not always required)
  • Set your hourly rate ($15-100+ depending on expertise)
  • Create a professional profile with your qualifications
  • Prepare lesson plans and teaching materials
  • Invest in a good microphone and webcam
  • Ensure a quiet, professional workspace

Best Tutoring Platforms

Chegg Tutors - Join Chegg Tutors

One of the largest tutoring platforms. Easy to get started, consistent student flow, pays $15-25/hour.

Tutor.com - Apply to Tutor.com

Established platform with wide subject range. Competitive pay and flexible scheduling.

Wyzant - Become a Wyzant Tutor

Specializes in one-on-one tutoring. Higher rates possible ($25-50+/hour) with good ratings.

VIPKid - Teach with VIPKid

Specializes in teaching English to Chinese students. $14-22/hour, flexible scheduling.

Preply - Join Preply

Language tutoring platform. Set your own rates and build your student base.

Care.com - Care.com Tutoring

General marketplace for connecting with local and online students.

Earning Potential

Beginners: $15 - $25 per hour

Experienced: $25 - $50 per hour

Expert/Specialized: $50 - $100+ per hour

Monthly Income Range: $500 - $5,000+ depending on hours and rates

Tips for Success

  • Be patient and adapt to different learning styles
  • Use engaging teaching methods and multimedia
  • Provide clear explanations and real-world examples
  • Create personalized lesson plans for each student
  • Track student progress and provide feedback
  • Ask for testimonials and maintain high ratings
  • Specialize in high-demand subjects (STEM, languages)
  • Offer flexible scheduling for working professionals
  • Continuously update your knowledge and skills

Subject Areas with High Demand

  • Math (Algebra, Geometry, Calculus)
  • Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • Languages (Spanish, French, Mandarin)
  • Test Prep (SAT, ACT, GRE)
  • Programming and Computer Science
  • English and Writing
  • Business and Professional Skills
